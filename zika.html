The Zika Virus is a virus that is transmitted mainly through Aedes mosquitos. It can also be transmitted sexually. The Zika virus is commonly found in tropical areas, such as Africa, South America, etc. Our website is dedicated to all persons affected by this virus, but our main concern is expecting mothers. If you are a pregnant or soon to be pregnant female living in an area where zika is prominent, our products are just for you! Your expecting child could be at risk for birth defects. A most common one is Microcephaly, which is an abnormal shrinking of the head.
